MACHINE MANUAL — PUMP-01 (Centrifugal Process Pump)
Version: 3.2  |  Site: Acme Manufacturing Plant, Unit B  |  Date: 2024-01

=============================================================================
1. MACHINE OVERVIEW
=============================================================================
Pump-01 is a horizontal-split-case centrifugal pump used to circulate cooling
water through the primary heat-exchanger loop. Rated flow: 450 L/min at
6.5 bar discharge pressure. Motor coupling: direct-drive, 37 kW, 1480 RPM
rated speed.

=============================================================================
2. NORMAL OPERATING RANGES
=============================================================================
  Temperature (bearing housing):  60–90°C
  Vibration (radial, RMS):        0.5–2.5 mm/s
  Discharge pressure:             5.5–6.5 bar
  Suction pressure:               1.8–2.5 bar
  RPM:                            1400–1520
  Motor current draw:             58–65 A

Warning thresholds:
  Temperature > 105°C — reduce load, check coolant flow
  Vibration > 3.5 mm/s — inspect mechanical seal and impeller balance
  Discharge pressure < 4.5 bar — check for cavitation or blocked suction strainer

Critical (shut down immediately):
  Temperature > 130°C
  Vibration > 6.0 mm/s
  Any sudden RPM drop > 200 RPM below setpoint

=============================================================================
3. MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
=============================================================================
Weekly:
  - Visual inspection for leaks at mechanical seal and flange joints
  - Record bearing temperature and vibration (log in maintenance book)
  - Verify suction strainer differential pressure < 0.3 bar

Monthly:
  - Grease bearing housing (2 shots, Mobilgrease XHP 222 or equivalent)
  - Check coupling alignment with dial indicator (max 0.05 mm angular)
  - Verify motor current draw under normal load
  - Flush drain plugs and re-seat

Annual:
  - Full strip-down and inspection of impeller, wear rings, and shaft sleeve
  - Replace mechanical seal assembly (P/N: GS-3142-SS)
  - Replace coupling insert (P/N: JAW-HRC-90-PU)
  - Hydrostatic pressure test at 1.5× design pressure
  - Motor winding insulation resistance test (min 100 MΩ at 500 V DC)

=============================================================================
4. TROUBLESHOOTING
=============================================================================

4.1 Overheating (Temperature > 105°C)
  Likely causes:
    a) Low coolant flow — check suction valve position and strainer blockage
    b) Cavitation — listen for rattling noise; increase suction pressure
    c) Bearing failure — check vibration; replace bearings if > 3.5 mm/s
    d) Shaft misalignment — re-align coupling
  Steps:
    1. Reduce pump speed by 10% via VFD and monitor temperature trend.
    2. Inspect suction strainer; clean if dP > 0.3 bar.
    3. If temp doesn't drop within 10 min, initiate controlled shutdown (§6).
    4. Replace bearings (P/N: SKF-6315-2RS) if noise or vibration is elevated.

4.2 High Vibration (> 3.5 mm/s)
  Likely causes:
    a) Cavitation — low suction pressure or air ingestion
    b) Impeller wear or damage
    c) Misalignment or loose coupling
    d) Resonance with support structure
  Steps:
    1. Check suction pressure; increase if below 1.8 bar.
    2. Reduce flow to 80% and observe if vibration decreases.
    3. Shut down and inspect impeller for erosion or debris.
    4. Re-check coupling alignment with dial indicator.

4.3 Low Discharge Pressure (< 4.5 bar)
  Likely causes:
    a) Worn wear rings (radial clearance > 0.5 mm)
    b) Partially closed discharge valve
    c) Air locked casing
  Steps:
    1. Confirm all valves are fully open.
    2. Vent the casing via the air vent valve on top of the casing.
    3. If pressure remains low, shut down and replace wear rings
       (P/N: WR-316SS-85mm).

6. SHUTDOWN PROCEDURE
=============================================================================
Controlled shutdown:
  1. Reduce VFD setpoint to 50% speed and hold for 2 minutes.
  2. Close discharge isolation valve slowly (over ~30 seconds).
  3. Stop motor via VFD stop command.
  4. Close suction isolation valve.
  5. Allow pump to cool to < 60°C before opening any flanges.
  6. Lock out / tag out (LOTO) per site procedure SOP-LOTO-002.

Emergency stop:
  1. Press red ESTOP button at local panel or in control room.
  2. Close suction and discharge valves manually if safe to do so.
  3. Report to shift supervisor immediately.
  4. Do not restart without maintenance sign-off.
